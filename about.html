About Our College

Shri. Mohanrao Murarirao Sawant Jr. College of Arts & Commerce and Shri. Tukaram Shivram Sawant Jr. College of Science, Kanedi is managed by the prestigious Kankavli Education Extension Board, Mumbai. Established to provide quality higher secondary education in rural Sindhudurg, we are committed to academic excellence and overall student development.

Principal's Message

Shri. S.D. Dalvi, Principal

"Our mission is to empower students from rural backgrounds with knowledge, skills, and values. With dedicated faculty and modern facilities, we prepare students not just for exams, but for life. We welcome you to be part of our family."

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence providing affordable, quality education and creating responsible citizens for tomorrow's India.
